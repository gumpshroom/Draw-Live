Draw Live! is a party game about drawing created by ggar.

To play, you need 5 people. Each game constitutes of a judge and two teams.
Once the judge picks a topic, the game starts. Each team has 1 minute to draw the topic.
To draw, members of a team take turns putting ink on the canvas to make shapes.
Each member will have 30 ink to draw every turn.
When the round is over, the judge will privately decide the victor of the round. Team names are not shown.
A team's score will increase when they win a round.
After the judge has decided, the game moves on to another round with a topic specified by the judge.
When 5 rounds have passed, the game will end. The team with the highest score will win the game.
